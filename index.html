Creamy Mushroom Lasagna

A delicious Italian classic with rich mushroom flavor and creamy texture

30 min
Prep Time
45 min
Cook Time
6
Servings
Medium
Difficulty

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets 12 pieces
  • Mixed mushrooms 500g
  • Onion 1 medium
  • Garlic cloves 3
  • Butter 50g
  • Flour 50g
  • Milk 500ml
  • Parmesan cheese 100g
  • Mozzarella cheese 200g

Seasonings

  • Olive oil 2 tbsp
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Black pepper ½ tsp
  • Nutmeg ¼ tsp
  • Fresh thyme 1 tbsp

Instructions

1 Prepare the Baking pan

Grease the baking pan with butter or oil, then sprinkle with citrus zest. You can also use lemon instead, or a mixed powder.

2 Preheat Lasagna

Pre-cook the lasagna sheets in boiling salted water. Soak them in a large bowl of ice water with a little olive oil. Then transfer them to a baking tray.

3 Assemble the Lasagna

Spread a thin layer of white sauce in a baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, then mushroom filling, then white sauce, and some mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and cheese on top.

4 Bake the Lasagna

Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 15-20 more minutes until golden and bubbly. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Cooking Tips

  • Use a mix of different mushrooms for more flavor
  • Let the lasagna rest before cutting to keep layers intact
  • Add spinach for extra nutrition and color
  • Leftovers taste even better the next day
